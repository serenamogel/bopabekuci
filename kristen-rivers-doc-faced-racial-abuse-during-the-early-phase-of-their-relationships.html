Kristen married her high school loves Doc Rivers.

The pair originally met in 1979 while attending Marquette University in Milwaukee. According to the source, Doc opened the conversation with Kristen by proposing that she wash her hair every now and again. The pair didn’t click right away, so Doc arranged for Kristen to meet one of his friends. Kristen dated his colleague for a decade before she and Doc began their new personal involvement. After dating for many years, they married in 1986.

They were subjected to racial abuse in the early stages of their relationships.

Kristen is white, and Doc is black. They fell in love while also dealing with many problems in their relationship throughout its early stages. Interracial dating was regarded as improper at the time, and as a result, they were exposed to racist abuse. According to the Orlando Sentinel, Kristen’s tires were slashed, and her parents’ house was spray-painted at the time.

His performance suffered as a result of the abuse. Despite the abuses and difficulties, they were able to maintain their connection. Aside from that, Doc was exposed to racism throughout his tenure with the Clippers. A mysterious fire broke out at Kristen and Doc’s San Antonia home, where they lived with their children. The fire was thought to have been started out of racial animosity.

Divorce is still pending.

The pair are divorcing after more than three decades of marriage. According to the insider, they have been separated for about six years and have been in the process of divorcing for the last three years. Doc, on the other hand, hasn’t made his dating status public.

Mother of four children

Kristen is the delighted mother of four children: son Austin, 28, daughter Callie Rivers, 32, son Jeremiah, 32, and youngest son Spencer, 25. Her children are all athletes, with all of her boys playing professional basketball and her daughter Callie playing volleyball. Austin was a professional basketball player with the New York Knicks of the National Hoops Association (NBA), while Jeremiah played basketball at Georgetown and Indiana before joining the Maine Red Claws. Spencer is presently a guard for UC Irvine.

Her daughter’s grand wedding

On September 14, 2019, her daughter Callie married Seth Curry, a professional basketball player for the Philadelphia 76ers. It was a beautiful wedding in Malibu, followed by a big celebration. Callie and Seth’s one-year-old daughter, as well as Seth’s two nieces Riley and Ryan, daughters of NBA star Steph Curry, acted as flower girls for the ceremony. The bride and groom hit the dance floor, making the event more exciting. Steph Curry was the best man.

Who is the mysterious lady in Doc’s life?

Doc seemed to have found a new lady in his life, despite the news of their impending divorce. According to reports, Doc placed his arms around his new woman’s waist outside San Vicente Bungalows in Los Angeles. However, no information about his new mystery girlfriend has been revealed.

Net worth

Kristen Rivers’s net worth is about $2 million as of December 2023 Doc has a successful coaching career. He has been the head coach since 1999. He began his coaching career with the Orlando Magic and afterward with the Boston Celtics. In 2008, he won the NBA title as the head coach of the Boston Celtics. He was the head coach of the Los Angeles Clippers from 2013 to 2020 before taking over as the head coach of the Philadelphia 76ers on October 3rd, 2020. As a basketball player and coach (annual compensation with The Clippers was $10 million), he signed a five-year deal with Philadelphia for a staggering $40 million, or about $8 million yearly.
