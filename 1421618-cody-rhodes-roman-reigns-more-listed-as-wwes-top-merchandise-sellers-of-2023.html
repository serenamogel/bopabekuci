Cody Rhodes, Roman Reigns, More Listed As WWEs Top Merchandise Sellers Of 2023

has unveiled its list of top-selling superstars of 2023, and in what should come as no surprise, two of the biggest superstars in professional wrestling headline the list.

The information comes from WWE Shop, and is headlined by both Cody Rhodes and Roman Reigns. The rest of the list also features some of the best superstars in WWE, as well as two extremely popular factions within the company.

WWE Shop’s Top Selling Superstars of 2023

The full list of WWE Shop’s top 10 selling superstars of 2023 can be seen below:

  • Cody Rhodes
  • Roman Reigns
  • LA Knight
  • John Cena
  • Rhea Ripley
  • Seth “Freakin” Rollins
  • The Bloodline
  • Judgment Day
  • Rey Mysterio
  • Kevin Owens

For those looking to pick up any items, the WWE Shop is also running a sale from now through November 14, 2023, where fans can get 25% off of their orders.
