This is from the Pokemon Black and White Quiz book :3 (This isn't my scan of it, but I do have it XD) It asks a question, gives three possible answers, but only one is correct.

A rough translation thanks to Jeska -

It's cut off in this picture, but the question at the top asks "What is the relationship between Araragi and Makomo?" (wHAT)

1) They were born on the same day
2) They were friends in grade school
3) They were classmates during studies (this is the correct answer)

I was really confused when I first read all of it, because I thought they were -all- right, but no, what is said in the games still stands. This is the nicest official art of them, too, in my opinion <3
