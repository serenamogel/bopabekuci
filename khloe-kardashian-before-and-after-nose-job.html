Khloe Kardashians Plastic Surgery Transformation | Her Before &amp; After Pictures

Khloé Kardashian has claimed that she has had plastic surgery multiple times in her life. While the reality star isn’t particularly forthcoming about her previous procedures, she has previously discussed the reasons for her decision to undergo surgery.

Khloé revealed on the reunion special episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians that she had made a few cosmetic changes. “Everyone says, ‘Oh my gosh, she’s had her third face transplant,’ but I’ve only had one nose job,” she said in the June 2021 episode. “And then everyone gets upset, like, why don’t I talk about it? Nobody has ever asked me.”

In addition to her nose job, the Good American founder revealed that she has had “injections” but “not really Botox.” I’ve had a terrible reaction to Botox.”

The reality star then revealed how she experienced body image insecurity while on set with her two sisters, Kim and Kourtney Kardashian — “I became insecure because of what everyone else was telling me.”

When Did Khloe Kardashian Have A Nose Job?

During the Keeping Up With the Kardashians reunion in June 2021, Khloe underwent surgery.
The actress admitted to the procedure on the show after host Andy Cohen asked her to clarify her much-discussed appearance.

Khloe did not say when she had the procedure done.

What Has Khloe Kardashian Said About Having A Nose Job?

“I’ve had one nose job—Dr. Raj Kanodia,” she said in an interview with Cohen. And everyone is upset. ” Why don’t I just talk about it? Nobody has ever asked me. You’re the first person to ever ask me about my nose in an interview.”

Following a previous negative reaction, Khloe revealed that she had “injections,” but “not really Botox.”

How Much Khloe Has Changed Through The Years

2010

Just three years after the premiere of KUWTK, Khloé was sporting dark brown hair and darker eye makeup to compliment her brown eyes.

2011

The only thing that seemed to change about the reality star this time was her hair, which she had dyed ombre.

2014

Khloé changed her ombre hairstyle to a set of blonde highlights and wore different eye makeup in 2014 to achieve a more natural look.

2016

Khloé kept changing her hair colors after revealing she had plastic surgery, but she wore neutral makeup for most red carpet events.

2017

By this point, the reality star was avoiding heavier makeup and opting for simple but sexy fashion choices when attending red carpet events.

2018

Khloé maintained her strong sense of fashion while sporting lighter blonde locks and began throwing her hair back in various styles.

2019

By 2019, Khloé wore her blonde hair down most of the time and used bold makeup to draw attention to her eyes.

2021

Khloé stayed true to herself in a sparkling Christmas Eve dress, ending the year with a new haircut.

2022

In February 2022, Khloé captioned a series of glammed-up selfies, including a video, “I rarely post in real-time… took this months ago.”

During a May 2022 episode of the “Not Skinny But Not Fat” podcast, KoKo addressed rumors that she had a face transplant, revealing that the comments used to bother her. “That sounds amazing later in life,” she said, “but right now, I’m fine.”
