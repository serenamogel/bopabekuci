Atiba Hutchinson- Wiki, Age, Height, Wife, Net Worth, Ethnicity, Career

Atiba Hutchinson (born February 8, 1983) is a Canadian professional soccer player who plays as a midfielder for Süper Lig club Beşiktaş and leads both the club and the Canadian national team. He was the first North American player to win the Danish Superliga Player of the Year title while playing in Denmark. Hutchinson received the first of six Canadian Player of the Year honors that season. He played for PSV Eindhoven in the Dutch Eredivisie from 2010 until 2013.
