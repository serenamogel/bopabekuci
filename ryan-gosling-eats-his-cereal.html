Ryan Gosling eats cereal as tribute to death of fan

Oh my, here is another reason to adore Ryan Gosling.

Once upon a time a guy called Ryan McHenry created a set of Vines of Ryan Gosling recordings from films and then tried to feed him cereal, which of course went viral pretty much overnight. Pretty genius right?

Until now, Ryan Gosling never responded.

In incredibly sad news, Ryan McHenry suffered from cancer and lost his battle earlier this week. The news of his death deeply saddened his online fans, and a petition was created on Reddit to ask Ryan Gosling to eat the cereal as it was McHenry's lifelong goal.

[vine id="OtwjQXVjmDr"]

Until now, that is.

In the hours of the early morning, Gosling posted this tweet of him actually tucking into some cereal as a tribute to McHenry, saying in a tweet: "My heart goes out to all of Ryan McHenry's family and friends. Feel very lucky to have been apart of his life in some small way."
