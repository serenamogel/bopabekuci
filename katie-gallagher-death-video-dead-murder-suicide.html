Katie Gallagher Career Highlights

Katie Gallagher was born to her parents in her hometown Pennsylvania, U.S. on 15 August 1986.

After her first year at the Rhode Island School of Design, Gallagher stated that she would major in garment design. 

The fashion designer’s first clothing design project at RISD was a garment constructed from her springs.

After migrating to New York City, the fashionist founded “Katie Gallagher”. The SS10 (spring/summer 2010) collection by Gallagher was started in preparation for the clothing’s debut at New York Fashion Week in September 2009.

Before production, Katie created drawings and sketches to develop the concept for the collection.

At New York Fashion Week, Gallagher debuted her SS10, AW10, SS11, AW11, SS12, and AW12 collections. Refinery 29 described her SS10 collection as darkly futuristic and sleek.

Among the materials used in the collection were boiled wool, shearling, leather, silk, and chiffon.

Daphne Guinness, Lady Gaga’s stylist Nicola Formichetti, and others have picked specific pieces from Katie Gallagher’s body of work for editorial shoots.

High-end shops worldwide have joined the list of Katie Gallagher’s line’s stockists. The Katie by Katie Gallagher diffusion collection, which features separates and jewelry, was introduced on November 5, 2012.
