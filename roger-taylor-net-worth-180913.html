Roger Taylor Net Worth

Roger Taylor is an English drummer born on April 26, 1960 in Nechells, England. He is best known for his work with the new-wave rock band Duran Duran, which he joined in 1978 and left in 1985, before rejoining in 2001. He has been a part of the band for over 40 years and is considered one of the most influential drummers of the new-wave era.
