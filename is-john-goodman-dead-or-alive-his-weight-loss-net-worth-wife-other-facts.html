Is John Goodman dead or alive?His weight loss, net worth, wife, other facts

John Goodman won the hearts of TV lovers with his performance in the ABC sitcom Roseanne , which aired from 1988 to 1997 and then in 2018. The actor and comedian cracked ribs with his weird mind, which earned him the 1993 Golden Globe Award for Best Actor. Since then he has been in tons of TV productions spanning movies, sitcoms and other shows.

You will surely remember him from the film adaptation of the animated series Flintstone which was released in 1994. Goodman first appeared on television in the ’80s and has remained relevant ever since. Not only is he known as “Dan Coner” in Roseanne, there’s a lot to know about the funny actor. Here are lesser-known facts about John Goodman, including his personal life and weight loss.

John Goodman’s bio

He was born on June 20, 1952 in St. Louis, Missouri, USA. He lost his father to a heart attack and grew up with his mother and siblings. At first, Goodman was fond of sports. He attended Affton High School, was involved in football and competed in school competitions. After graduating in 1970, he received a football scholarship and transferred to Southwest Missouri State University, where he continued to play football. However, his football dreams were shattered after he sustained an injury that prevented him from playing.

All hope was not lost for Goodman; he soon switched to studying theater, where he met notable figures such as Kathleen Turner and Tess Harper. After graduating, he moved to New York to look for better opportunities. He started out with voice acting and commercial jobs before appearing on Broadway’s Loose Ends .

In 1982, he landed his first film role in the film Eddie Macon’s Run That has garnered some positive reviews. A number of other films followed, and the actor gradually gained prominence. He appeared in the Broadway musical Big River in 1985 before landing another major role in the comedy True Stories the following year.

In the late ’80s, Goodman had a major role in the sitcom Roseanne (1989-1990), which was considered the most-watched program in America at the time. He also acted in a few Coen Brothers films, some of which included; Barton Fink (1991), The Big Lebowski (1998), Oh Brother Where Art Thou? (2000) and others. He went ahead to make appearances on shows like Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip and The Western Wing, all directed by Aaron Sorkin; and also got a starring role in Flintstones (1994).

The 2000s brought more promising roles for the actor. He was cast in Coyote Ugly The film is based on the chain of bars Coyote Ugly Saloon in the US. He was cast in the film Beyond the Sea which was based on Bobby Darin’s life. In 2013, the actor received the prestigious title “Disney Legend”, and also played the character of the North Carolina Senator in the comedy Alpha House . In 2018, Goodman made a return for the revival on ABC alongside some of the original cast of Roseanne .

Wealth (income and earnings)

John Goodman is sitting on an enviable net worth of $65million as estimated by many sources. The actor and comedian made a whopping $250,000 per episode during the revival of the acclaimed Roseanne series. Though the talented actor doesn’t fuss about his financial situation, it’s nothing new to the public that he’s enjoying a comfortable life with his family.

Personal life – wife, children

Contrary to his noisy career, John Goodman has common sense and an admirable personal life, which he does not always successfully hide from the media. A traditional family man, Goodman is married to his wife Annabeth Hartzog, with whom he has tied the knot since 1989. Goodman’s marriage is also a source of inspiration for other entertaining couples in the industry. They have one daughter, Molly Evangeline together.

weight loss

Goodman’s story cannot be told entirely without a mention of his weight loss journey. The actor, who weighed 400 pounds in the previous Roseanne series, lost a streak of over 100 pounds in the reboot. How did he survive the journey he started in 2015? First, Goodman had to cut down on some things like alcohol and junk. He also employed a personal trainer – Mackie Shilstone – who introduced him to workable exercise routines. Goodman started from there, training six days a week to reach his current height.

Is John Goodman dead or alive?

Death rumors are common among celebrities, but what? if there’s a shred of truth in them? What if Goodman really was dead? Reports of Goodman’s death made headlines in 2017 after a revival of the Roseanne series was announced.

The most common question making rounds on the internet was what about the dead John Goodman? Goodman’s character (Dan) died in the previous series, but his character was brought back to life in the reboot. As of 2018, Goodman is still alive in real life.
