A Teacher movie review &amp; film summary (2020)

published on March 05, 2024
“A Teacher” presents Claire’s behavior without excusing it, and the miniseries pulls a sort of bait and switch. During its first half, “A Teacher” almost lulls you into accepting Claire and Eric as a “normal” relationship, with the only problem being that Claire is married. Quyen Tran’s cinematography is a recurring reminder that we’re watching something criminal unfold, with shots that place us as witnesses: spotting through the smeared glass of a gym door Eric following Claire to her car for their first rendezvous; seeing in split screen the increasingly inappropriate text messages they send each other.
