Maria Tumarkin is an Australian cultural historian. She is also the author of four idea books: Traumascapes (2005), Courage (2007), Otherland (2010), and Axiomatic (2018). She has taught at universities and writing centers and is a Ph.D. Candidate in cultural records on the University of Melbourne. She moved to Australia from Russia when she became fifteen years old.
