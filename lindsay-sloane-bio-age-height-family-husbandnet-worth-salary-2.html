Lindsay Sloane Biography

Lindsay Sloane (Lindsay Sloane Leikin) is an American actress best known for playing Valerie Birkhead on Sabrina the Teenage Witch (1997–99) and Emily in The Odd Couple (2015–17). Sloane has also starred in the following films; Bring It On (2000), Over Her Dead Body (2008), She’s Out of My League (2010), The Other Guys (2010), Horrible Bosses (2011), and its sequel Horrible Bosses 2 (2014).
