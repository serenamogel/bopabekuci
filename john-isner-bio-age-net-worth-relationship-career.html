John Isner | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

John Robert Isner, more commonly referred to as John Isner, is an American professional tennis player. In July 2018, following his quarterfinal appearance at the 2018 Wimbledon, Isner, who turned professional in 2007, achieved a career-high ranking of No. 8.

Over five years, he dominated the United States in tennis. As of 2021, he has amassed 15 singles titles and 5 doubles titles.

Notably, in 2018, he advanced to the semifinals of Wimbledon, the highest round of play in any Grand Slam competition. To date, he has amassed fourteen titles on the ATP Tour, including his inaugural Masters triumph at the 2018 Miami Open.

Throughout his lengthy career, he has held numerous records, including the ATP record for the quickest “official” serve ever, which he achieved in 2016 at 157.2 mph.
