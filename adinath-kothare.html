Adinath Mahesh Kothare, popularly known as Adinath Kothare is a multi-skilled person who is blessed with additional skills like, acting, directing, and producing. He is basically from India, belongs to a Hindu family, and born on 13th May 1984, Mumbai. He has strong roots in the Marathi cinema as he is the son of the most acclaimed Marathi actor, director. The young actor has achieved so much during his career life and hoping to get a more desired future by working in various movies.

Career

Adinath Kothare initially took the start of his career with Pritish Nandy Communications Ltd. Where he worked as an associate executive producer. Also, he has worked as a management trainee with PVR in film marketing. And you will be amazed to know that he has been so passionate actor since childhood as he appeared in the movie named “Maza Chakula” as a child star. It was directed by Mahesh Kothare and released in the year 1994. In the year 2013, he worked in the movie which was directed by his father. The film “Zapatlele 2” which rocked at the box office. It was the first 3D movie in the Marathi cinema. While he also appeared in the movie “Ishq Wala Love” which was directed by Renu Desai. Meanwhile, he marked his identity in the Hindi cinema and appeared in Sanjay Sarkar’s directorial. It was about a sports drama “Stand By”. In 2011, he also worked in the film “Duhbang which was directed by Mahesh Kothare. However, he has acted in various movies and got much acclaim for his extraordinary contribution to the Marathi cinema. Apart from acting, he also produced 2 movies including “3 Dhamal” and “Zapatlele 2” in 2013, both were directed by his father. Also, he and his father run 2 production houses.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Adinath Kothare was honored with the Maharashtra State Award, Zee Gaurav, and Sahaydri Cine Award.
