Jackie Goldschneider Bio (Wiki)

Who is Jackie Goldschneider?

Jackie Goldschneider is a reality TV actress. Goldscneider is renowned as a member of the Bravo TV series, The Real Housewives of New Jersey.

Jackie Goldschneider is also a freelance writer, newspaper columnist, and former lawyer.

What are the birth facts of Jackie Goldschneider?

Jackie Goldschneider was born on the 2nd of October in 1976 in the United States. She is of British-Irish ancestry.

Jackie- Professional Career

She started her career as a US attorney. Later, she left her job as a lawyer and moved to TV for her career expansion as a TV personality.

In 2016, she first appeared in the short movie, The Domenick Nati Show as a guest. Two years later, she was cast in the TV series, Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen. 

Following the show, she appeared in the reality TV show, The Real Housewives of New Jersey in the ninth season. The show becomes a milestone in her career as a TV personality. Currently, she is associated with the series. As of now, the TV actress has appeared in the 34 episodes.

Apart from TV, she also writes for magazines and newspapers.
