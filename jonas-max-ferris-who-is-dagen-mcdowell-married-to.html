Jonas Max Ferris: Who is Dagen McDowell married to?

Dagen McDowell is married to Jonas Max Ferris. Jonas Max Ferris is an American Financial adviser, journalist, and economic analyst.

Jonas Max Ferris is also a panellist on Fox Business Network and a producer known for If the Trees Could Talk (2014). Jonas Max Ferris is an economic analyst and financial adviser who attended the University of Georgia.

Dagen McDowell is an American business news anchor, TV personality, accountant and co-host of The Bottom Line on Fox Business. She is also a contributor to Fox News.

Dagen McDowell had served as a panellist on Cavuto on Business, which airs on Saturdays. She took over hosting Bulls and Bears in 2016.

In December 2022, Fox Business named Dagen McDowell co-host of a new show called The Bottom Line alongside Sean Duffy. The program is expected to debut on January 23, 2023.

Dagen McDowell also appeared on Imus in the Morning prior to Imus’ death in 2019 and was a weekly panellist for Cashin’ In.
