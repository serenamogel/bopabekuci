Is Gangsta Boo married? Who is the rappers boyfriend?

Gangsta Boo was not married at the time of her death but was in a relationship with her boo, Emmet Flores.

The Three 6 Mafia member, whose real name was Lola Mitchell, reportedly passed away on January 1, 2023, at the age of 43. She was found dead in her hometown of Memphis on January 1, 2-23, around 4 pm.

Who is Gangsta Boo’s boyfriend?

The late rapper’s boyfriend, Emmet Flores is well known for being the podcast’s host, “Kush and Chemtrails.”

On his official Instagram profile, where he primarily advertises his podcast, he has amassed over 1,508 followers.

Recently on WEtv’s Marriage Boot Camp: Hip Hop Edition program in 2022, the duo discussed their relationship in detail, giving fans a rare chance on knowing what’s up with them. The couple discussed their relationship issues in front of Judge Lynn Toler in one episode.

In March 2022, Gangsta Boo and Emmet made an appearance on the new season of Marriage Boot Camp: Hip Hop Edition.

The rapper stated that she and her lover had been dating for eight years, starting in 2014, when speaking with T-Storm and Mike Swift of the Unpopin Show on YouTube.

They were honest with Judge Lynn Toler about their relationship problems while they were on the program in an effort to make things better.

At the time of writing this article, Flores had not publicly addressed the death of his now-late girlfriend on social media.
