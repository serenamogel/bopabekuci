Shop Selena Gomezs Baggy Jeans Affordable Dupe

Another day, another Selena Gomez meme scouring the internet — and we’re not mad about it. Gomez recently attended the Los Angeles FC versus Inter Miami soccer game at BMO Stadium, when her reaction to Lionel Messi’s near goal became a pivotal pop culture moment. Messi was swiftly blocked by the Los Angeles FC goalkeeper, John McCarthy, in an extremely close-range shot that left fans shocked. Like most of the stadium, Gomez’s eyes widened, and jaw dropped for an instant viral meme.

While fans took to the reaction and ran with it to Twitter, we were stuck thinking about those baggy jeans she posted a mirror selfie with before the big game. Gomez wore a set of gold earrings, a white flowy halter top, oversized boxed jeans, and crisp white sneakers (plus a wrist cast, which she most recently revealed to have broken by tripping in a long dress on the way to her car).

As the baggy jeans trend makes its way back thanks to Gomez and is presumably safe from another broken bone incident, we found a dupe that you can snag for the upcoming fall season. Abercrombie & Fitch—another throwback brand that’s finding its way back onto our credit cards — sells the Curve Love High Rise Wide Leg Jean for only $90. The pants come in black, light wash, and medium wash — but make sure to act fast because the medium wash is almost sold out online! Long are the days of fitted tops and tight jeans.

In the event that the Abercrombie jeans do sell out, there’s a secondary dupe for you. You can purchase the UQJE Women’s Wide Leg Jeans Baggy Denim from Amazon for half the price — $44.99 — in black, light blue, and dark blue. What’s the upside of these trendy pants? They look extremely comfortable with a high waist that is actually scrunchie — not buttoned like the usual jean style. The singer has a track record of single-handedly bringing back nostalgic fashion — remember her OOTD with that 2000s-inspired long denim skirt?! This is also not the first time that Gomez has become a viral meme, as she just made headlines a few weeks ago for that far-away look photo featuring a cozy Mexican blanket.
