Claire Harris Obituary,  Death – My heartfelt condolences go out to the Pets 2 Places family on the loss of Nic, who was a much-loved part of the team. It is never easy to say goodbye to a loved one, but it can be especially difficult when that person was highly valued and committed to the position they played. Everyone who worked at Pets 2 Places must have been profoundly influenced by Nic’s contributions, as well as by her affection for the customers as well as the fuzzy animals she cared for on a daily basis.

It is never easy to lose a coworker, and my thoughts and prayers are with Nic’s family as well as all of the members of the Pets 2 Places community who are going through a difficult period right now. Everyone is going through a challenging time right now, therefore it is critical that we stick together and offer support to one another. Pets 2 Places was initially established by Claire Harris.

I have no doubt that your leadership was a significant factor in the establishment of a working environment in which individuals like Nic were able to flourish and feel a strong connection to the work that they do. Please accept my sincere condolences, and may you find the fortitude to lead the organization through this challenging time. The people whose lives she touched on a regular basis will carry on Nic’s legacy by remembering her for the difference she made in their lives.

Her unwavering commitment and undying affection for the part she played will be fondly recalled and appreciated by everyone who had the honor of getting to know her. During this trying time, I want Nic’s family, friends, and coworkers to know that my thoughts and prayers are with them. Please don’t hesitate to let us know if there is anything the community can do to help her family or to commemorate her legacy. We would really appreciate it. May Nic rest in peace, and may the light of her legacy continue to shine brightly through the relationships and work that she has left behind.
