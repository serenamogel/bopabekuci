Found: The Best Moveset For Incarnate Thundurus in Pokemon GO | Thundurus Best Moveset

In Pokemon GO, Thundurus is one of the three Forces of Nature. Its popularity has skyrocketed since its Therian Forme is now a 5-star raid boss in the game.

Each of the djinn-like Pokemon has an Incarnate Forme opposite their Therian Forme. The stats of these forms are different.

Trainers in Pokemon GO, on the other hand, will want to use Incarnate Thundurus’ best moveset if they want to be successful. When it doesn’t required to win a fight of durability, Incarnate Thundurus has an incredibly high attack stat, making it a good choice in Ultra and Master League PvP.

Picking the best moveset for Incarnate Thundurus in Pokemon GO


Incarnate Thundurus as it appears in the Pokemon Trading Card Game (Image via The Pokemon Company)
Between its Fast and Charge moves, Incarnate Thundurus may learn six different moves in Pokemon GO.

Astonish and Thunder Shock are its Fast Moves, while Crunch, Thunder, Brick Break, and Thunder Punch are its Charge Moves.

The PvP moveset of Incarnate Thundurus can also be used in PvE combat. It’s a good idea to use Same Type Attack Bonuses (STAB), which occur when a Pokemon uses a move of the same type as it.

Incarnate Thundurus’ optimal moveset has a lot to recommend it. Trainers will want to use Thunder Shock and Thunder Punch as the Pokemon’s primary moveset for the most part. This is due to the fact that both moves grant STAB, and Thunder Punch is a reliable Charge Move. It doesn’t deliver as much damage as Thunder, but its energy economy is significantly superior.

Because Incarnate Thundurus lacks defense and endurance, it’s better to use speedier Charge Moves rather than harder-hitting ones.

Crunch is a good choice for trainers looking to offer Incarnate Thundurus a secondary Charge Move.

While Thunder is a powerful move that does a lot of damage, the Pokemon may not have enough time to replenish its energy reserves. Furthermore, it is an Electric-type move, and if trainers are already employing Thunder Punch, the Legendary Pokemon’s type efficacy is reduced.

Crunch should give Incarnate Thundurus a lot more options when it comes to fighting Psychic-type Pokemon. When utilizing moves that match its own types, this provides it a subtle usefulness that it wouldn’t have otherwise.
