Who Is Trent Reznor?, Birthday, Family, Albums, Social Media

Trent Reznor Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings, Net Worth: Trent Reznor, officially known as Michael Trent Reznor was born on May 17, 1965, in New Castle, Pennsylvania.

He’s a well-known American musician, singer, songwriter, record producer, and composer who has risen through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after acts.

Reznor showed an early aptitude for music at a very younger age and began playing the piano at the age of 12.

In the mid-1980s, Reznor started his career as a member of synth-pop bands including; Option 30, The Innocent, and Exotic Birds.

He serves as the lead vocalist, multi-instrumentalist, and principal songwriter of the industrial rock band Nine Inch Nails.

Reznor founded the industrial rock band Nine Inch Nails in 1988 and was the sole official member until 2016.

In 1997, Reznor appeared on Time’s list of the year’s most influential people, and Spin magazine described him as “the most vital artist in music”.

He has contributed to the albums of artists including Marilyn Manson, whom he mentored, rapper Saul Williams and Halsey, to mention a few.

Since 2010, Reznor, alongside Nine Inch Nails collaborator Atticus Ross, began to work on film and television scores.

The duo have scored in many of David Fincher’s films including; The Social Network, The Girl With The Dragon Tattoo, Gone Girl, and Mank, among others.

They won the Academy Award for Best Original Score and the Grammy Award for Best Score Soundtrack for Visual Media for both The Social Network and Soul

The duo, Reznor and Atticus Ross has also scored the films; Patriots Day, Mid90s, Bird Box, Waves, Bones And All, and Empire Of Light.

They have scored the documentaries; Before The Flood, The Vietnam War, and the TV series Watchmen, winning a Primetime Emmy Award for the lattermost.

Trent Reznor Birthday

Trent Reznor was born on May 17, 1965, in New Castle, Pennsylvania, United States. He celebrated his 58th birthday on Wednesday, May 17, 2023.

Trent Reznor Family

Parents

Trent Reznor was born in New Castle, Pennsylvania, United States to his parents; Nancy Lou (mother) and Michael Reznor (father).

He’s of German and Irish descent. His parents divorced when he was six years old therefore he lived with his maternal grandparents while his sister, Tera lived with their mother.

Wife

Trent Reznor is married to Mariqueen Maandig who was born on April 5, 1981. It’s unclear when the pair met. However, they tied the knot in October 2009.

Mariqueen Maandig is a Filipino-American singer, songwriter, and musician. She’s the vocalist for How to Destroy Angels and the former vocalist of Los Angeles-based rock band West Indian Girl.

Children

According to reports, the 58-year-old singer, songwriter, record producer, and composer has been blessed with five children.

However, only three of his children’s name was available at the time of this writing. They are; Nova Lux Reznor, Balthazar Reznor, and Lazarus Echo Reznor.

Trent Reznor shares all five children with his wife, Mariqueen Maandig whom she married in October 2009. The entire family lives in Los Angeles.

Siblings

Trent Reznor is not the only child of his parents; Nancy Lou (mother) and Michael Reznor (father). He has a sister called Tera. After their parent’s divorce, Tera lived with their mother while Trent went to live with his maternal grandparents.

Trent Reznor Albums

At the time of this writing (Wednesday, May 17, 2023), Trent Reznor together with the band, Nine Inch Nails have released twelve studio albums. They are; Pretty Hate Machine (1989), Broken (1992), The Downward Spiral (1994), The Fragile (1999), With Teeth (2005), Year Zero (2007), Ghosts I–IV (2008), The Slip (2008), Hesitation Marks (2013), Bad Witch (2018), Ghosts V: Together (2020), and Ghosts VI: Locusts (2020).
