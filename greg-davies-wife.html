Greg Davies Wife – Greg Davies is a Welsh comedian, actor, presenter, and writer. He is best known for his roles as Greg in We Are Klang, Mr. Gilbert in The Inbetweeners, Ken Thompson in Cuckoo, Taskmaster in Taskmaster, Dan Davies in Man Down, and Paul Wickstead in The Cleaners the latter of which he also wrote.

He has appeared as himself on Mock the Week, Fast and Loose, Live at the Apollo, and Would I Lie to You?

For his performance in Cuckoo, Davies was nominated for the 2013 BAFTA Television Award for Best Male Comedy Performance.

Wiki, Age, Bio

Davies was born on 14 May 1968 in St Asaph, Denbighshire, Wales. He is 54 years old as of 2022. He is well educated. Davies was educated at Thomas Adams School in Wem, before studying English and drama at Brunel University in London. Before starting a career in comedy, he taught drama for 13 years at several secondary schools: Langleywood School in Slough, Orleans Park School in Twickenham, and Sandhurst School in Sandhurst.
