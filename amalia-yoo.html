Yoo was eighteen when she landed on the Netflix teen drama Grand Army. She made her TV debut in this series as Leila Kwan Zimmer, and it became widely popular. Despite being new to the industry, she received massive success and love from viewers and film critics just like Emma Batiz.

Yoo was able to show her acting skills in the series, which is why she soon started getting many opportunities to work in other series & movies like Obliterated and The Other Zoe. No doubt, the actress has able to become one of the Hollywood sensations at a very young age.
