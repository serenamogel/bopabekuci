Larry pleaded guilty to his crimes in 2017 and 2018, sentencing him to 60 years in federal prison and 40 to 175 years in state prison. Then as per the orders of a judge, his sentence was extended to life imprisonment.

Along with the team doctor, the females blamed the entirety of the system that allowed the abuse. FBI also got thrashed for their slow response in Nassar’s case. Further, the FBI fired one of the two FBI agents who lied to cover their error.
