How much does Aryna Sabalenka make?

Aryna Siarhiejeŭna Sabalenka is a Belarusian professional tennis player.

The twenty-four-year-old tennis player has achieved a career-high ranking as world No. 2 in singles and world No. 1 in doubles by the Women’s Tennis Association (WTA).

How much does Aryna Sabalenka make a year?

Aryna’s current prize money is US$ 10,035,560.

Sabalenka has won two Grand Slam doubles titles, the 2019 US Open, and the 2021 Australian Open, alongside Elise Mertens.

Sabalenka’s style of playing is known to be aggressive. She often accumulates high numbers of winners and unforced errors. With her height, she also has a very strong serve.

Aryna Sabalenka net worth

Aryna has a net worth of $50 million as of February 2022.

Aryna began playing on the ITF Women’s Circuit in 2012. Her first five tournaments were in her hometown of Minsk and spread out over two years, but she won her first professional match in Istanbul in the later part of 2014.
