Lamar Odom Children: Meet Jayden Odom, Lamar Odom Jr, And Destiny Odom

Lamar Odom is an American reality TV star and former professional basketball player who competed in reality shows like “Dancing with the Stars.” Lamar Odom is a two-time NBA champion, a one-time NBA Sixth Man of the Year, and a one-time NBA All-Rookie First Team.

Lamar Odom Children: Meet Jayden Odom, Lamar Odom Jr, And Destiny Odom

Lamar Odom is blessed with three children: Jayden Odom, Lamar Odom Jr, and Destiny Odom. All the children of Lamar Odom were born to an American fashion designer called Liza Morales.

Jayden Odom is one of the sons of Lamar Odom. Jayden was born on December 15, 2005, and died on June 29, 2006, after seven months of birth.

Lamar Odom Jr is one of the sons of Lamar Odom. Lamar Odom Jr was born in 2002, hence, he is 20 years of age.

Destiny Odom is the daughter of Lamar Odom. Destiny Odom was born in 1998, hence, she is 24 years of age.
